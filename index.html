About Me

Hello World

My name is Morrison Key, but please call me Key. I am a product designer and a previous full-stack web developer based in Berlin. I have experience with User Centered Design and am well versed in many programming languages and frameworks.

I have always had a desire to create. I can't tell you how many laser adorned play-dough dinosaurs I made when I was a kid. There rarely is a time that I am not cooking up some harebrained idea in my head.

My decision to become a designer grew from my belief that computer applications are the final frontier of invention. Think about it! Thanks to modern technology, we now have the ability to take an idea from inception to completion in only a few days and using nothing more than a computer. It really is a wonderful life! I pursued my passion and now I build cool things.
