About Me

Hello World

I am Keyan, a full-stack web developer based in New York City. I have experience with Object Oriented Programming and am well versed in Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Javascript and many JS libraries/frameworks. Recently, I graduated from the Web Development Immersive program at General Assembly, a 12-week program that teaches both the back-end and front-end skills necessary to build custom CMS and interactive web applications. You really can learn a lot when you spend 12 hours a day coding for three months!

I have always had a desire to create. I can't tell you how many laser adorned play-dough dinosaurs I made when I was a kid. There rarely is a time that I am not cooking up some harebrained idea in my head. My decision to become a developer grew from my belief that computer programming is the final frontier of invention. Think about it! Thanks to modern technology, we now have the ability to take an idea from inception to completion in only a few days and using nothing more than a computer. It really is a wonderful life! I pursued my passion and now I build cool things.

Since sharpening my skills at General Assembly's WDI program I have worked on several different projects for individuals and start-ups. Most recently I completed a contract with Imperative Group where I worked to build a site to help professionals find purpose in their work (www.imperative.com). Now it is my imperative to find new opportunities that give me purpose and I know that I will find them in this industry.
